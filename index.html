A Place to Worship, Grow and Belong

Porters House Community Church Worcester is a Christ-centered family dedicated to worshiping God, teaching His Word, serving our community, and helping people discover their purpose through Jesus Christ.

Whether you're visiting for the first time or looking for a church family, we invite you to join us as we grow in faith, hope, and love.
