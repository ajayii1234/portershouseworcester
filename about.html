Welcome to Porters House

A Church Where Faith Comes Alive

Porters House is a vibrant family of believers passionate about worshipping God, teaching His Word, and transforming lives through the power of Jesus Christ. We believe church is more than a building— it is a community where everyone is welcomed, loved, and encouraged to grow in their relationship with God.

Whether you are visiting for the first time, searching for a church home, or looking to deepen your faith, you will find a place where you can belong, serve, and flourish.

Our Beliefs

What We Believe

Our faith is firmly rooted in the Holy Scriptures and centered on the life, death, resurrection, and Lordship of Jesus Christ.

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible and authoritative Word of God. It is our foundation for faith, doctrine and Christian living.

Jesus Christ

We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God, born of the Virgin Mary, who died for our sins, rose again on the third day, and now reigns forever.

Salvation

Salvation is a free gift of God received through faith in Jesus Christ alone and not by human works.

The Holy Spirit

We believe the Holy Spirit empowers believers for holy living, spiritual growth and effective ministry.

Prayer

We believe prayer is essential in every believer's life and that God hears and answers according to His will.

Our Journey

Our Story Through the Years

From humble beginnings to a growing church family, every season has been a testimony of God's faithfulness and grace.

2008

Church Founded

Porters House began with a small gathering of believers committed to prayer, worship and the teaching of God's Word.

2012

First Permanent Worship Centre

We dedicated our first worship centre, creating a place where families could gather and grow together.

2016

Community Outreach Expansion

Our outreach ministries expanded into schools, hospitals, orphanages and underserved communities.

2020

Digital Ministry

We launched online services, reaching people around the world through digital platforms.

Today

Growing Together

Today we continue to raise disciples, serve our community, and share the love of Christ with people everywhere.
