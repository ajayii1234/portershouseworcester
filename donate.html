Biblical Stewardship

Why We Give

Giving is an act of worship and gratitude to God. Through your generosity, lives are transformed, communities are served, and the Gospel reaches more people.

Advance the Gospel

Your giving helps spread the message of Jesus Christ through worship services, evangelism, discipleship, and church planting.

Support Missions

We partner with missionaries and local outreaches to reach communities with the hope and love of Christ.

Care for People

Your generosity enables us to support families, provide relief to those in need, and minister to vulnerable members of our community.

Build God's House

Donations help maintain church facilities, improve worship spaces, and create an environment where lives can be transformed.
